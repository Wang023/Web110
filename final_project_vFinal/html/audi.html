The exterior of the AUDI RS7 is bold and aggressive, showcasing its motorsport roots.
It features a distinctive vertical kidney grille, aerodynamic air intakes, and a lightweight
carbon fiber roof that lowers the center of gravity. The wide fenders and sharp body lines enhance its athletic stance,
while the quad exhaust system and rear diffuser highlight its performance capabilities.
Optional packages like the M Carbon Exterior Package add additional lightweight carbon accents
on the mirror caps, front splitter, and rear spoiler,further emphasizing its track-inspired design and aerodynamic efficiency.

The interior of the AUDI RS7 combines luxury with a clear focus on the driver.
It features M Carbon bucket seats that offer excellent support during spirited driving, upholstered in
premium Merino leather. The cabin is filled with carbon fiber trim, aluminum accents, and customizable
ambient lighting for a refined, high-performance atmosphere. A 12.3-inch digital instrument cluster
and 14.9-inch curved infotainment display provide seamless access to vehicle functions through BMWâ€™s latest iDrive system.
From its driver-centric layout to the race-inspired detailing, the interior offers a perfect blend of
comfort, technology, and precision.

The the AUDI RS7 is equipped with a 3.0-liter BMW M TwinPower Turbo inline-6 engine (S58),
delivering an impressive 503 horsepower and 479 lb-ft of torque. Engineered for high performance,
it features twin mono-scroll turbochargers, high-pressure direct injection,
and a forged lightweight crankshaft for rapid response and durability.
Paired with an 8-speed M Steptronic automatic transmission and available M xDrive all-wheel drive,
this powertrain enables a 0 - 60 mph time of just 3.8 seconds. The result is a perfectly balanced blend of speed,
precision, and everyday drivability true to BMW Ms racing heritage.
