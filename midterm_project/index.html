Who I am

Hello! I'm Gane, I am 22 years old,
A passionate web developer and designer with a strong foundation in programming and digital creativity.
My journey into tech started after earning my Baccalauréat in Experimental Sciences in Algeria in 2020.
Driven by curiosity and a love for problem-solving, I pursued a degree in Mathematics and Computer Science
at Mouloud Mammeri University, where I built a solid understanding of programming,
computer architecture, and how machines interpret code.
In 2021, I discovered the world of web development, which quickly became my passion.
Over the next two years, I honed my skills, working on multiple projects that strengthened
my expertise in front-end and back-end development.
By 2023, I had successfully built my first full-stack web application,
solidifying my commitment to creating seamless and visually compelling digital experiences.
After this transformative journey, I took the next big step: moving to the United States to further my education.
I am now a proud Cavalier at Johnson County Community College (JCCC), where I continue to grow as a developer,
explore new technologies, and refine my design skills.
I'm always eager to push creative boundaries, collaborate on innovative projects,
and bring ideas to life through code and design...
